Air

Forests are vital to life on Earth. They purify the air we breathe, filter the water we drink, prevent erosion, and act as an important buffer against climate change.

The area of primary forest worldwide has decreased by over 80 million hectares since 1990.

This poses a great threat to Humanity and life on earth itself.

The Ocean






Covering 70 percent of the Earth's surface, the ocean transports heat from the equator to the poles, regulating our climate and weather patterns.

At least 14 million tons of plastic end up in the ocean every year,threatening and worsening climate change

The need for Ocean conservation is now paramount.

Do Donate or partake in the amazing effords led by these teams.

  • Oceana-A non-profit international Ocean advocacy organization
  • Global Coral Reef Alliance-An initiative to save the reefs
